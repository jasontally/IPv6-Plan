How to Use

Enter an IPv6 address and click Go to load.

Click green Split buttons to divide subnets.

Click red Join buttons to merge subnets back together.

Add notes and colors to document allocations.

Click Share to copy the URL with current state.

Click Export CSV to download subnet plans.

Why use this tool

  • Nibble-aligned splitting divides networks at 4-bit boundaries for easy hexadecimal management
  • Custom split targets support geographical denomination models and specialized addressing schemes
  • Prefix range from /16 to /64 with /64 as minimum
  • Location-based planning shows /48 counts for large allocations and /64 counts for smaller subnets
  • Visual hierarchy join buttons span rows to show parent-child relationships
  • Annotations document allocations with notes and colors
  • Shareable URLs encode complete state for sharing
  • CSV export download plans with hierarchy indentation
